About

Tic Tac Grow v1.0
Ties grow the board!
Score points for chains of 3+ on grids 4×4 and larger.

How to Play
Goal
Outscore your opponent when the timer ends.
The Grid Grows
On a 3×3, classic rules apply — first to 3-in-a-row wins!
On a tie, the board expands to 4×4, then 5×5, and so on.
Scoring (4×4+)
3-in-a-row → 10 pts
4-in-a-row → 20 pts
5-in-a-row → 30 pts
6-in-a-row → 40 pts
Each extra cell adds 10 more pts.
Undo
One undo per game. Use it wisely!
Zoom
Use +/− buttons (or pinch on mobile) to zoom the board. Zoom out to place marks.
